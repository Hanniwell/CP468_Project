9 years ago The nasal cavities empty into the? Answer Save 1 Answer Relevance Autumn Rose 9 years ago Favorite Answer Your nasal cavities empty into the pharynx, or throat. Basically, you have the external portion of your nose, which leads you to the nasal cavity. This nasal cavity connects the outer portion of your nose to your pharynx (throat).
